Why it matters

It turns Bookmarks into a map of trusted internet ecosystems.

Instead of only listing resources, Bookmarks becomes a place where expertise, taste, context, and identity shape discovery. That makes the experience more human, more niche, and far more memorable than a plain directory.

Human context

Links feel stronger when users understand why they were selected and who stands behind them. Creator stacks provide narrative, trust, and editorial framing.

Better discovery

A person-driven browsing model helps users reach precise subcultures and useful niches more quickly than broad taxonomy alone ever could.

Creator value

It gives creators a clean public page for their best resources, with attribution, direct traffic, and a better way to organize the useful internet around their field.

Designed for official collaboration, not lazy scraping.

Bookmarks aims to feature creators through voluntary contribution, creator-approved selections, or assisted curation workflows that stay respectful, editable, and clearly attributed.

Official attribution: every collection can carry clear creator credit and profile links.
Flexible input: exports, simple lists, docs, spreadsheets, or rough drafts are all workable.
Editorial control: creators can request edits, updates, or removal at any time.
